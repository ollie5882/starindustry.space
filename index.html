// Who We Are

Introducing
Star Industry

Star Industry is built to solve a fundamental problem facing the new space sector: the supply chain cannot keep up. Not enough suppliers can produce space-grade components at scale, and critical engineering expertise — particularly in flight safety and mission analysis — remains dangerously scarce across Europe.

We are a vertically integrated company, combining specialised engineering consultancy, advanced composite hardware, and a long-term vision for in-space transportation. Our team are former senior leaders from Orbex, with direct hands-on experience of the assets, programmes, and customers that define this market.

Our model is designed to generate near-term revenue from day one — while laying the foundations for the next generation of in-space mobility.

// Tier 1

Engineering Services

Our consultancy capability covers the full mission lifecycle — from concept and trade studies through to licensing, safety assessments, and operational risk analysis. Flight safety capability independently recognised as "Best in Class" by the UK regulator.

🛸

Mission Architecture & Analysis

Mission design, trajectory optimisation, performance trade studies, and delta-v analysis. Supporting launch vehicles, kick stages, and orbital transfer missions from concept to operational phase.

🔭

Flight Safety & Launch Licensing

End-to-end support for launch vehicle licensing: casualty expectation analysis, debris modelling, trajectory safety envelopes, and regulator documentation. With you for every mission, not just the first.

📋

RAMS & Safety Cases

Reliability, availability, maintainability, and safety analysis across complex launch systems. Hazard analysis, reliability allocation, safety case development, and operational safety concepts.

🏗️

Structural Design Consultancy

Structural analysis and design for launch vehicle components, composite structures, and pressure systems. Deep expertise in cryogenic CFRP and high-pressure vessel design.

📊

Investor Technical Due Diligence

Independent technical evaluation of launch startups and space companies for investors. Architecture assessment, performance validation, risk registers, and development roadmap reviews.
